About

Hello, my name is Scott Jones and I am an aspiring developer based out of Victoria British Columbia Canada. I bought my first computer so that I could record music and have since developed an interest in how computer programs and websites function. During the Covid-19 pandemic I used my time off work to start exploring programming and have since pivoted my career path to the technology industry. I am currently enrolled in the Interactive Media Developer program at Camosun College. I look forward to gaining some work experience and expanding my knowledge in this field.

Some of my interests are music, video games, film, illustration, mountain biking, and snowboarding. Music is my biggest hobby. I write and record my own electronic music that I post on the web. You can listen to my work here.

I am inspired by the intersection between art, music, design and technology. My goal is to amalgamate my creative disposition with my growing programming and design skills to create beautiful and functional websites and applications.
