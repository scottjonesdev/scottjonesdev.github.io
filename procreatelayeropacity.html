Layer Opacity in Procreate

November 20, 2020

Procreate had an update earlier this fall that changed the location of where you can toggle the opacity of the various layers in your project. After the update I could no longer find this control. After spending a fair amount of time stumbling around the app I discovered the new location of the opacity control slider. The new location actually makes a lot more sense than where it was before. The opacity slider is now attached to each layer directly. You just need to tap the ’n’ to access it. See the image below for the more details.
